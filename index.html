Rules

  • Registration Fees Rs. 200/- per Team

  • Every Team will submit the idea solution in the prescribed template on or before 11 th June 2021.

  • Round 1 will be on 12 th & 13 th June 2021 10.00 am onwards. Every team will have 07 mins for presentation and 02 mins for Q&A. The shortlisted teams will be communicated next day. These shortlisted teams will start coding to deliver the solution.

  • Evaluation Round will be on 26 th and 27 th June 2021 from 9:00 am to 6 pm. In this round, Evaluators will assess solution periodically (after 4 hours) and will provide suggestions. Team shall incorporate alterations/additions suggested by evaluators during assessment.

  • All teams will share code on GitHub. Final Version will be considered for evaluation.

  • Results will be announced on 30 th June 2021.

Problem Statements
1. An Online System for Mentoring Students of Education Institute.

Develop a Responsive web application / Mobile application for online Mentoring of Students of an Educational Institution.
In routine life, students’ are not always able to get the perfect answer to their queries and questions raised, though they take efforts to search on the internet, use a video or tutorial. When a student has any issue with their studies or academics, he/she usually contacts faculty but if the respective faculty is unavailable, it creates havoc for the student to solve the problem. With the help of this app- MENTOR MATE, students will post their query and the faculty members will respond to it with the answer and pin pointed notes given to the student. This makes things easy for students to learn using these resources. In case a student is unable to understand the reply or the answer given by the faculty, he can request for a one-to-one meeting with the faculty that makes the situation easy for the student to learn the topic more easily. It is an observation that all other learning platforms only provide tutorials and examples, but this app will target to provide a facility to have a one-to-one meeting of the student with their concerned mentor.
2. Exam Monitoring System.

Develop a Desktop application for an Online Examination Monitoring system (OEMS), which will be used to monitor an examination conducted in an online mode. OEMS will register running processes on the clients. Whenever an exam starts, the monitoring system logs the processes that will run on the computer with all popup messages. Log will also contain Login time, Logout time, idle time more than prescribed time etc. OEMS will stop automatically when the exam stops and generate reports including an examination score, individual student log and other analysis parameters concerned with an exam. The system shall send 3 alerts in case of malpractice and will automatically end the exam if such malpractice is continued. Log shall also capture all the pop-ups.
Develop a system to discourage a student from cheating and check the content of the computer clipboard (copy/paste-function).
3. Dashboard For T&P Activities.

Develop a dashboard for T&P activities happening at institute level for an academic year.
Application shall have various modules to facilitate students’ registration, display current placement drives & Results, Scheduled Training plans, facility to add/delete e-Resources, Assessment Schedules, placement statistics (institute level, department level and at individual level) and display Individual Students’ Performance.
Application shall have the facility of importing data from excel/csv files.
